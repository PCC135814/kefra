Typical Dutch Food


The stroopwafel. This is the most known Dutch cookie.
It contains a caramel sauce in the middle with two thin waffles on each side. Enjoy!


Dutchies usually eat breakfast and lunch similar to this picture.
Chocolate drops on bread.


The bitterbal is a dutch small ballshaped croquette.
Usually the filling is made out of meat.


These are little pancakes served with powdered sugar and butter.


It's a Dutch tradition to eat this treat when a baby is born.
Blue for a boy, pink for a girl.

The most know dinner for Dutch families is this dish called boerenkool.
Roughly translated as "farmer's cale" this dish is eaten
with mashed potatoes and smoked sausage.
